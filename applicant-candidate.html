Applicant and Candidate Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1st 2025

At Royal Monarch Fintech Private Limited ("Royal Monarch"), we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle personal data collected during our recruitment process.

1. Scope of This Policy

This policy explains:

  • What information we collect during the application and recruitment process;
  • How and why we use your information;
  • With whom we share your information;
  • Your rights regarding your personal information.

Note: Use of Royal Monarch’s services is also subject to our general Privacy Policy and applicable terms.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Details: Name, address, email, phone number, and contact details.
  • Application Materials: Resume, cover letter, transcripts, work history.
  • Interview Notes: Observations from interviews or screenings.
  • Job Preferences: Role, location, salary, relocation info.
  • Referral Details: Source or person who referred you.
  • Demographic/Sensitive Info: Gender, nationality, disability, ethnic origin (as needed or shared).
  • References & Background Checks.
  • Online/Public Info (e.g. LinkedIn).
  • Assessment Results (if applicable).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Evaluate your skills and qualifications;
  • Conduct interviews, checks, and communicate with you;
  • Consider you for future roles (unless requested otherwise);
  • Legal/regulatory compliance & diversity tracking;
  • Immigration support (if needed);
  • Protect Royal Monarch’s rights and safety.

If hired, data becomes part of your employee record.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (if applicable)

  • Contractual necessity;
  • Legal obligations;
  • Legitimate interests in recruitment;
  • Consent (e.g., referees or future role consideration).

5. Sharing Your Information

  • With Royal Monarch affiliates globally;
  • Internal team/referrers;
  • Service providers under confidentiality;
  • Government/regulators if required;
  • With consent (e.g., references).

You are responsible for getting referee consent before sharing their contact details.

6. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed outside your home country, including in India. Safeguards are in place wherever it's processed.

7. Data Security

Technical and organizational measures like encryption, secure storage, and access controls are used to protect your data.

8. Data Retention

If not selected:

  • We may retain your info for future roles or legal obligations;
  • You may request retention/removal via hr@royalmonarch.in.

9. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data;
  • Object to certain uses, depending on laws.

Contact: hr@royalmonarch.in

10. Automated Decision-Making

We do not use automated systems to make hiring decisions—humans are always involved.

11. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated. The latest version is posted here with its effective date.
