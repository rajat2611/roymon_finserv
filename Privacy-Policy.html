Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Royal Monarch Fintech Private Limited ("Royal Monarch") operates the website www.royalmonarch.in (the "Website"). Royal Monarch is committed to protecting your privacy and using technology that ensures a safe, secure, and efficient online experience.

We collect and use your information to provide a personalized web experience, enhance our service delivery, and enable our sales and marketing teams to connect with the appropriate audience. We only request information that we believe is essential to achieve these objectives.

2. Information We Collect

  • System Data: We use your IP address for diagnostics, website management, and analytics.
  • Registration Data: Name, email, PAN, PIN code, age, income level.
  • Order Forms: Contact, financial (credit card, account), and demographic information.
  • Surveys and Contests: Used to personalize content and communication.
  • Third-Party Ads: May place cookies; not controlled by Royal Monarch.
  • Supplemental Info: From 3rd parties to improve service accuracy.

3. Sensitive Personal Data (as per IT Rules, 2011)

Publicly available or legally disclosed data is excluded.

4. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect data to comply with KYC norms, customize services, and meet regulatory standards. Consent withdrawal must be submitted in writing. Data is deleted when no longer needed per legal norms.

5. Data Security and Protection

We implement robust security practices and protocols. While we strive to protect data, full internet security cannot be guaranteed. In case of a breach, Royal Monarch will demonstrate safeguards implemented.

6. Disclosure of Information

  • Sensitive data only shared with protected third parties
  • Clients may request access to their own data

Information posted in public forums (chats, boards) is public. Use caution when posting personal details.
